Pizza

Description

The pizza is one of the most famous dishes of all time. It is also delicious and very easy to follow up.

This recipe has the perfect mix of cheddar, mozzarella, cream cheese and pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Pepperoni
  • Cream Cheese
  • Pesto

Steps

  1. Give the pizza dough the desired form.
  2. Put the marinara sauce on the dough.
  3. Slice the mozzarella cheese in small chunks.
  4. Put the chunks in the dough with some space between them.
  5. Shred the cheddar very thin and add it to the pizza.
  6. Spread the cream cheese all over the dough.
  7. Add the pepperoni to the pizza.
  8. Add pesto and the olive oil to one's taste.
  9. Bake the pizza.
  10. Enjoy!
