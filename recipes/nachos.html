Nachos

Description

Make quick, delicious and easy nachos at home in just minutes!

This mouth-watering recipe is incredibly simple to make and everyone is going to be happy.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Sliced jalapenos

Steps

  1. Pour the tortilla chips into a bowl.
  2. Add melted cheddar into the bowl.
  3. Add the sliced jalapenos.
  4. Enjoy!
